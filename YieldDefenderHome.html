Yield Defender helps you get the most out of every acre

Our legacy has been built on providing ag producers an effective and efficient method of increasing yields and maximizing your return on every acre.

For over 40 years we have been building purpose built aerial spray planes to help meet the world’s food, fuel, and fiber needs. Being true to our founder's vision of innovation. We are bringing our expertise of aviation and agriculture into building unmanned aircraft systems (UAS).

Yield Defender allows you to make smart data-driven decisions for each of your farms. Together, we collect and analyze field data, measure performance and build prescriptions.
